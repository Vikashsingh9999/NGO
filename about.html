About ANSS

A home of hope for children who need a family.

Anath Niradhar Sevabhavi Sanstha (ANSS) was founded in 2021 with a simple promise: every child deserves safety, dignity, education, and love.

Support a child today Visit / Volunteer

Our Story

Founded with compassion, built with trust

ANSS began with founders Santosh Aher and Sheetal Aher, who saw children facing life without a stable home. With limited resources but strong community support, a safe shelter became a caring home.

Today, we focus on three outcomes: a safe place to live, consistent education, and emotional wellbeing. We work with local supporters, volunteers, and partners to keep children healthy, learning, and hopeful.

Mission

Provide holistic care—shelter, nutrition, education, healthcare, and guidance—so every child can thrive.

Vision

A society where every child—regardless of origin—has equal opportunity, dignity, and belonging.

Our Values

  • Care — every child is treated like family.
  • Safety — a secure environment and responsible processes.
  • Transparency — clear communication and impact reporting.
  • Community — working with supporters, donors, and volunteers.
