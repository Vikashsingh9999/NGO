Our Story

A Haven of Hope Established in 2021

Anath Niradhar Sevabhavi Sanstha was born from a simple yet powerful realization: no child should have to face the world alone. Founders Santosh and Sheetal Aher started this journey with limited resources but unlimited compassion.

Located in the heart of our community, ANSS has grown from a small shelter into a loving home where orphaned children find safety, education, and most importantly, a family.

Our Mission

To provide holistic care including shelter, nutrition, and education to every orphaned child.

Our Vision

A society where every child, regardless of origin, has equal opportunities to thrive.

What We Do

Holistic Child Development

We go beyond basic needs. Our programs are designed to nurture the physical, emotional, and intellectual growth of every child.

Education Support

Ensuring every child attends school, receives tuition support, and has access to learning materials.

Shelter & Nutrition

Emotional Counseling

Professional counseling to help children heal from trauma and build mental resilience.
